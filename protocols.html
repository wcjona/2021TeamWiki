POLYMERASE CHAIN REACTION (PCR)

  1. Combine in a 0.2ml microcentrifuge tube:
    5l of NEB 10X standard Taq buffer (final concentration = 1X)
    0.25l of NEB Taq (final concentration = 1.25U/50l
    1l of 10M forward primer (final concentration = 0.2M)
    1l of 10M reverse primer (final concentration = 0.2M)
    1pg to 1ng of template plasmid DNA (final concentration < 1000ng/l)
    1l of 10mM Kapa dNTPs (final concentration = 200M)
    ddH2O to 50l
  2. Vortex 2 to 3 seconds to mix, then centrifuge briefly to settle
  3. Place in thermocycler and select or set up the appropriate program as follows:
    Initial denaturation -> 95C for 30 seconds
    Repeat 25 to 30x:
    Denaturation -> 95C for 15 to 30 seconds
    Annealing -> Tm - 5C (45 to 68C) for 15 to 60 seconds
    Extension -> 68C for 1 minute per kilobase
    Final extension -> 68C for 5 minutes
    Hold at 4C
